# Stay Safe Online - Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone, no matter who they claim to be.
2. Always remember that entering your UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.
3. Do not trust unknown links or payment requests sent on WhatsApp or SMS.
4. Always double-check the identity of the person asking for money.
5. Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown or suspicious sources.
6. Turn on notifications for all transactions so you are aware of any activity.
7. Use strong passwords and do not reuse the same password everywhere.
8. Keep checking your bank account regularly for any unusual transactions.
9. Do not install apps from unknown sources or random links.
10. If you face any fraud, report it immediately on cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
