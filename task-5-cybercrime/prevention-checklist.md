# Stay Safe Online – Cybercrime Prevention Checklist

1. Never share your UPI PIN with anyone under any circumstances.
2. Remember: Entering UPI PIN means you are sending money, not receiving it.
3. Do not click on unknown or suspicious payment links.
4. Always verify the identity of the person requesting money.
5. Avoid scanning QR codes from unknown sources.
6. Enable SMS and app notifications for all transactions.
7. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication on all accounts.
8. Regularly check your bank statements for unauthorized transactions.
9. Never install unknown apps from untrusted sources.
10. For any fraud, immediately report it on https://cybercrime.gov.in or call 1930.
